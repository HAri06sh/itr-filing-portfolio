ITR Filing - All You Need to Know (AY 2025–26)

What is ITR?

Income Tax Return (ITR) is a form to declare income, expenses, deductions, and taxes paid to the government during a financial year.

Who Should File?

  • Individuals earning above ₹2.5 lakh annually
  • Businesses and professionals
  • Freelancers and gig workers
  • Anyone with foreign income/assets
  • Those who want a tax refund

Important Dates

  • Individual: 31 July 2025
  • Business Audit Cases: 31 October 2025

Types of ITR Forms

  • ITR-1: Salaried (< ₹50L)
  • ITR-2: Capital gains, >1 house
  • ITR-3: Business income
  • ITR-4: Presumptive tax

Required Documents

  • PAN, Aadhaar
  • Form 16, 26AS
  • Investment proofs
  • Rent receipts, bank statements

Deductions (Save Tax)

  • 80C: LIC, PPF, ELSS
  • 80D: Health Insurance
  • 80G: Donations
  • 80E: Education Loan Interest

How to File

Use incometax.gov.in for e-filing or consult professionals like us.
